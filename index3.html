World Hunger's Impact On The World


How Has World Hunger Impacted The World?

World Hunger has impacted the world in various ways, hunger has caused conflict, and conflict is the primary driving force in the world. It uproots families, destroys economies, ruins infrastructure and halts agricultural production. Hunger has displaced people, including refugees and asylum seekers. Many suffer from hunger but children under the age of 5 who are especially children under the age of 5 are prone to growth problems and malnutrition, along with diseases greatly affects their immune system.

How Has World Hunger Impacted People?


The ones marginalised in a community, such as elderly women, displaced poeple and refugees, and people with disablilities are more likely to face food insecurity and illness including malnutrition. Hunger also causes significant mental consequences to growth in children, malnourished children can experience cognitive delays and difficulty with concentration. Chronic malnutrition can lead to health problems like anemia, blindness and brain damage.
